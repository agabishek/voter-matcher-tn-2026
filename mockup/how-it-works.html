🧭 How Voter Matcher Works

Everything explained simply — how questions are prepared, how your answers are scored, and how results are generated. No jargon.

0 The Big Picture

📋 You answer 30 questions → ⚖️ Mapped to 9 belief dimensions → 🎯 Party match percentages → 🧠 Your voter profile

You answer questions about real policy trade-offs. The system maps your beliefs to 9 dimensions of political thought, then shows which party's positions most closely match yours.

1 How Questions Are Prepared

Step 1: Identify Real Policy Trade-offs

Each question presents a genuine dilemma that Tamil Nadu voters face — not hypothetical scenarios.

"If the government has a limited budget, what should be the priority?"

Option A: Give cash directly to families · Option B: Build hospitals and schools · Option C: Create jobs through private investment

These are based on actual manifesto positions and governance decisions of DMK, AIADMK, and TVK.

Step 2: Domain Experts Assign Weights

Political analysts study each party's manifesto and track record, then assign a weight (0–5) to each answer option for each party:

Weight 0 = this party strongly opposes this position. Weight 5 = this party strongly supports it.

2 How Your Answers Are Scored

The Scoring Pipeline

Your 30 Answers → Sum Party Weights → Normalize to 100% → DMK 58% · AIADMK 28% · TVK 14%

Worked Example

Say you answered 3 questions and picked these options:

QuestionYour ChoiceDMKAIADMKTVK
Budget priorityCash to families243
Corruption approachIndependent watchdog415
Language policyTamil-first in schools534
Raw Total11812
Normalized %35.5%25.8%38.7%

Formula: (party raw score ÷ total of all parties) × 100 = percentage. Always sums to exactly 100%.

Confidence Score — How Strong Is Your Match?

The gap between your top party and second party determines confidence:

🟢
High
Gap > 20 points
Your beliefs clearly align with one party
🟡
Medium
Gap 10–20 points
Moderate alignment, some overlap
🔴
Low
Gap < 10 points
Your beliefs span multiple parties

The formula adapts to party count: High = 100/N + 12, Medium = 100/N + 2. With 3 parties: High > 45%, Medium > 35%.

3 How Your Voter Profile Is Built

Archetype Classification — Your Political Personality

Based on which belief dimensions scored highest, you're classified into one of four voter archetypes:

🛡️ Security Seeker

Welfare + Poverty

Prioritizes government support and economic security for families

⚖️ Equity Builder

Social Justice + Governance

Prioritizes systemic equality and institutional reform

🔧 System Reformer

Corruption + Economy

Prioritizes transparency, accountability, and economic reform

🗣️ Identity Advocate

Language + Federalism

Prioritizes Tamil identity, cultural preservation, state autonomy

⚠️ Important: Your archetype is based on your belief dimensions, not your party match. A Security Seeker could match any party.

Contradiction Detection — Nuance, Not Error

If your answers show strong interest in two opposing dimensions (e.g., maximum welfare AND minimum government), the system flags this as a nuanced perspective — not a mistake.

ℹ️ "Some of your answers reflect nuanced or cross-ideological views. This is common and shows the complexity of your political thinking."

When contradictions are detected, confidence is reduced by one level (High → Medium) to reflect the ambiguity.

4 How the Explanation Is Generated

After scoring, the Explanation Engine assembles a plain-language summary from config data — no hardcoded text:

1. Primary match — "Your responses align most closely with [Party] (58%)" — references your top scoring axes in plain language
2. Secondary insight — "You also show 28% alignment with [Party]" — explains partial overlap
3. Belief statements — 2–4 tags like "You prioritize social justice" — derived from your highest axis scores
4. Archetype description — 2–3 sentences explaining your voter personality type
5. Consistency check — If contradictions detected, framed as "nuanced perspective" not "error"
6. Track record notice — If matched party is new (TVK), notes alignment is based on promises not governance record

All text is generated dynamically from config files. Switch language and everything re-renders in Tamil or English instantly.
